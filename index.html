Foreword

This booklet presents a comprehensive overview of skill development, awareness, and entrepreneurship programmes conducted by MSME DFO Srinagar. Spearheaded by Sh. Saheel Yaqoob Alaqband and Sh. Mohammed Tariq Ishtiaq, the branch empowered artisans, entrepreneurs, and youth across Kashmir under schemes including PM Vishwakarma, EAP, ESDP, and IPR initiatives.

Acknowledgement

The branch extends sincere gratitude to Sh. Sajid Hussain Shah, Young Professional, for his invaluable contribution in coordinating logistics, documentation, on-ground execution, and timely reporting. His commitment played a vital role in the successful implementation of these programmes.
